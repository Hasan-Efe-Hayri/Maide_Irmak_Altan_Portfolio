01 — About

Sound is how
stories breathe.

I am Irmak, a senior Digital Game Design student who is passionate about creating new worlds through sound. At the same time, I am pursuing a minor in Piano at the Conservatory and working as a game music composer.

I have composed music for many games through the numerous game jams and projects I have participated in. In addition to this, I create new atmospheres through sound not only for the game industry but also for various cinematic and listening experiences.

My goal is to build a career primarily in game music production, composing, and sound design.
