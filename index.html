01 — About

Sound is how
stories breathe.

I'm a third-year Digital Game Design student at Bahçeşehir University, with a deep passion for crafting audio experiences that transform how players feel inside a game world.

From composing adaptive loop-based soundtracks to implementing audio in FMOD and Wwise, I bridge the gap between creative vision and technical execution. I'm also a conservatory piano minor student, keeping my musical roots sharp.

My goal is to build a career in game music production and sound design — creating audio that doesn't just accompany a game, but defines it.
